Executive Summary

This report argues that UK marginal-cost pricing structurally benefits wind assets; UKW’s diversified 2 GW portfolio converts this into inflation‑linked dividends (~9% yield) with prudent leverage and buybacks supporting NAV, while key risks are wind variability, rates, and policy.
